Dara Petroleum Company

Dara Petroleum Company (PetroDara) was established in 2000, as a joint venture On behalf of EGPC, Dublin, & others. According to the law no. 15 of 1998. Trans Globe Energy Corporation acquired all of the shares on 2007/2008. PD is Operating in The Eastern Desert, about 17 Km north of Gharib

PetroDara (PD) have Four producing fields (Hana/Hana West, Hoshia, Arta & East Arta field) the cumulative oil production reached up to 37 million barrels & the total recovery reserve reached 47 MMBBLS by end of fiscal year 2017/2018.

North West Gahrib (NWG) Company was established in Dec-2016 according to the law no. 1753/2016 under PetroDara Board Members, the concession area is close to PetroDara concession area. NWG have three producing fields (NWG-1, NWG-2 & NWG-3), the cumulative oil production reached up to 642 thousand barrels & the total recovery reserve reached 2.2MMBBLS by end of fiscal year 2017/2018. The company set a goal to reduce the company spending the total cost reduction during 2017/2017 FY reached 1.6 MMEGP without considering dollar rate change & diesel price change.
