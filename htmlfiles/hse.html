Dara Petroleum Company

The main aim of the company’s safety is to maintain the secureness and safeness of the employees and instruments and reduce incidence of injuries and accidents; in addition to the interest in protecting the environment from any risks that may result from the nature of the operations in fields and also the occupational health of workers who are at different sites.

First:Health & Safety Achievements:

  1. Achieved 398 working days without any loss time injury.
  2. Completed High Priority Installations for Field Earthing System.
  3. Completed of Unified HSE Management & Manual System by a specialized company in this field; Implementation on going.
  4. Completed up grade HSE Management system software with WB and TG.
  5. Held Safety stand-down event on Tuesday dated 28 /11/2018 “safety day”.
  6. Joint Tender “PD & WB” for ISO Certification – Cairo & Field.
  7. Preparation of emergency plan for flash floods.
