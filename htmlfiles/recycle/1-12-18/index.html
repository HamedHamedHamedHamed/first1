Exploration

PetroDara drilled two development wells (Arta-48 and Arta-54) in Arta Development Lease to produce oil from Sandy Limestone of Nukhul Formation with a success ratio of 100%. The two wells add Original oil in place about 2.071 MMBO and recoverable reserve about 0.663 MMBO.

Operation

PetroDara located on Gulf of Suez about 17Km from north of Gharib city and the company started production on 2000 with avg production rate about 1500 BOPD,and reached max production during Fiscal year 2012/2013 with avg daily production rate 12,400 BOPD 2017/2018 fiscal year production reached 1.9 million barrels,with an average daily production rate of 5,220 BOPD achieving 80% of company plan (6,500 BOPD)
