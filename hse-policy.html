HSE POLICY

All management & employees are committed to:

First


  1. Meet or exceed all applicable Laws, Rules, Regulatory Standards, Requirement’s or follow International Standard where those do not exist or are ambiguous.


  2. Provide Company’s employees with a safe, healthy and environmentally friendly workplace, and to not to expose employees’ to health hazards through the protection from work related injuries.


  3. All persons have the power to STOP the JOB when any HSES risk is identified.

HSE POLICY

Second:

  1. Target zero harm to people and assets through risk management and application of precautionary measures.
  2. Protect the environment and surrounding communities, and limit the effect of harmful substances on them by minimizing emissions, waste disposals and energy consumption.
  3. Maintain and activate emergency response plans to control emergency situation.
  4. Consult the local community to ensure its traditions and heritage are maintained.
  5. Ensure all employees, contractors, visitors and other concerned parties understand and follow this QHSES policy.

Safety is everyone’s responsibility.
